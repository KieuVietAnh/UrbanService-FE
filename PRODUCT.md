# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Residents reporting urban issues from their phones, service staff triaging and resolving cases, field technicians executing repairs in the field, interaction managers monitoring service quality, and administrators configuring workflows and permissions.

## Product Purpose

UrbanMind provides a modern smart-city operations platform that connects resident reports to local government services, streamlines case management, and delivers clear status visibility across reporting, assignment, execution, and review.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, authoritative, approachable. A premium government-technology product that feels calm, modern, and service-focused, with a tone that is confident, clear, and supportive.

## Anti-references

Not a generic admin template. Avoid Bootstrap-style dashboards, noisy gradient-heavy UI, student capstone aesthetics, purple gradients, excessive shadows, nested cards, and visual clutter. Do not look like a consumer social app or a flashy startup landing page.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize clarity by surfacing the next action, current status, and important context without distraction.
- Build trust through transparent, data-driven status and consistent information hierarchy.
- Support mobile-first workflows so reporting, review, and resolution work well on smaller screens.
- Keep surfaces restrained and purposeful, using space and emphasis intentionally rather than decoration.
- Make user roles and workflows obvious so residents, operators, technicians, and managers quickly understand their view.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA at minimum, with strong support for readable contrast, keyboard navigation, mobile touch targets, and reduced-motion preferences. Design should feel accessible and dependable for a broad range of users.
